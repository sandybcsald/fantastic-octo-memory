About the bootcamp

This Bootcamp is to help students in becoming Industry-ready by taking the first step toward learning & growth. We develop a system which works at a foundation level. No Long term commitments, just 2 days of complete learning and hands on training on live projects with a touch of mentorship sessions. It emphasizes more on developing curiosity and making you to learn the principles of web development all by yourselves rather than spoon feeding and leaving you helpless at the end of bootcamp. Since most of the students are in dilemma of their future there is a personal mentoring session for every member of bootcamp to guide and show the path towards sustained excellence. Even after the completion of BootCamp we are to help you out in every possible way we can.
